# Clinical-Navigator (NanayCare v.1)
### A RavenBill-RDHS Research & Development Project
**"Bridging the Gap in Climate-Resilient Pediatric Healthcare"**

## 🏥 Overview
Clinical-Navigator is an **offline-first (PWA)** decision-support engine designed for mothers and frontline health workers in Geographically Isolated and Disadvantaged Areas (GIDA) in the Philippines. By digitizing official clinical protocols, we eliminate "healthcare guesswork" during climate-induced infrastructure collapses.

## 🚀 Frontier Tech Features (UNICEF Area 4 Alignment)
*   **Offline-First Architecture**: Built with **Vanilla JS** and **Dexie.js (IndexedDB)** to ensure 100% functionality without internet.
*   **High-Logic Algorithmic Triage**: Strictly mapped to the **WHO-IMCI (Integrated Management of Childhood Illness)** protocols.
*   **Edge Computing**: Processing life-saving data locally on the user's device to ensure speed and resilience.
*   **Zero-Trust Privacy**: MVP is private-by-design; no patient data is transmitted to the cloud, ensuring total compliance with the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012.

## 🌦️ Climate Resilience Impact
Climate change accelerates disease surges (Dengue, Malaria, Diarrhea) in the Philippines. During typhoons or floods, connectivity fails. Clinical-Navigator remains functional, providing a "Point-of-Care" bridge for mothers when traditional health systems are unreachable.

## 🛠️ Technical Roadmap (Current Phase: MVP)
RavenBill-RDHS is currently refactoring the "Logic Lab" to move from prototype to production:
1.  **Refactoring**: Transitioning from AI-assisted rapid prototyping to a secure, modular architecture.
2.  **Encryption**: Implementing **Web Crypto API** for AES-256 local-device data security.
3.  **Anonymized Sync**: Developing a background-sync layer for high-level epidemiological trend mapping.
