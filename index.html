Medical Disclaimer

The following information provides general guidance on pediatric medication dosing and is intended for informational purposes only. It does not replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Medication dosages for children are typically calculated by healthcare professionals based on body weight (mg/kg), the type of infection, and its severity. Doses are often divided into multiple administrations per day. Special considerations apply to infants under 3 months of age due to developing kidney function.

In more severe or resistant infections, higher dosing regimens may be required under medical supervision. Accurate dosing must always be determined by a qualified healthcare provider.

Do not self-medicate or adjust dosages. Always consult a licensed pediatrician before administering any medication to a child.
